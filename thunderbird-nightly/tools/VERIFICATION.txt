This package is automatically updated by the Chocolatey AU (Automatic Update) framework.

HOW TO VERIFY THE BINARY:
-------------------------
1. The downloader URL template is:
   https://ftp.mozilla.org/pub/thunderbird/nightly/latest-comm-central-l10n/thunderbird-{version}.{lang}.{arch}.installer.exe

2. You can manually download the file and verify its SHA256 checksum against the one provided in the 'tools/checksums.txt' file within the package.
   Get-FileHash -Path "path_to_binary" -Algorithm SHA256

The checksums are extracted from Mozilla's official Nightly manifest during the automated build process.
